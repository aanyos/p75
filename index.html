Campus Life

School church

Was established in 1963

The history of Holy Child Parish reflects the history of East Regina's growth and development. Originally, the Catholics of the area attended Little Flower Parish, but by the early 1960's, the people of the growing Glen Elm subdivision worked together to establish St. Joseph the Worker Parish, with their new church officially blessed on September 2, 1963.



School Library

Was built in 1980

The St. Theresa School Library is a modern, quiet space to read and study combining classroom teachings and inspire lifelong learning. Preschool visits each week for a read-aloud story time and can check out age appropriate books.

School Auditorium

What's New

  • New School Canteen

    Operating times

    The canteen is open every day during school terms from 9am until 12pm. Lunches can be ordered via Flexischools every day (an online ordering system) or paper bags (Monday to Thursday only). lexischools orders can be placed in advance and regular orders can be scheduled. Flexischools can also be used to purchase tickets to school functions such as discos. A class representative brings lunch orders to the canteen at the beginning of the school day. Orders are then collected by a class representative at the start of lunchtime. During the morning the canteen is open for over-the-counter sales of fresh fruit. We are open at lunch until 12pm for over-the-counter sales of fresh fruit, snacks, drinks and cold treats.

    Canteen menu

    Our canteen is a member of the Healthy Kids School Canteen Association and follows the Department of Education’s NSW Healthy School Canteen Strategy. We are also nut-free. The canteen price list is available online on this website. We ask that if you write lunch orders on a paper lunch bag, don’t forget your child’s name, class and the money. Paper bags are available from the canteen for 20c.

  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    SchoolPass after school program software is seamlessly integrated with daily attendance, dismissal plans, and your SIS. Activity coordinators always have an up-to-date after school program attendance roster. Checking students in and out of after school programs is fast, easy, and safe. Any unexpected absences are immediately reported the front office and optionally to parents. At check out, SchoolPass authorizes and captures signatures for each student pickup.
